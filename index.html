Welcome! My name is Li Peng, born in the Henan Province, China. I am a PhD student, studying in the UESTC (University of Electronic Science of China).

My research interests lie in distributed optimization. I've researched how to adopt the distributed method to solve the (EDP) economic dispatch problem in the large-scale power system. The EDP aims at seeking an optimal stategy to minimize the production cost, while it satisfying the production capacity constraint of plannt, and a coupled constraint. Recently, I am interested in the EDP subject to the uncertainty. As we know, the uncertainty is extensively existed in the practiced case. If we deal with the EDP without considering the uncertainty, the obtained solution maybe a suboptimal solution, what is worse, it isn't feasiable. Feel free to look at my publication and my project to learn more!
